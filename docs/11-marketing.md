# 11 — Marketing Playbook

## Brand Positioning

### Core Positioning Statement
**"GOTT adalah blockchain garbage protocol pertama yang dibangun di Indonesia. Kami membersihkan sampah blockchain — dan membayar lu untuk itu."**

### Brand Personality
- **Pragmatic:** Fokus utility, bukan hype
- **Local:** Bahasa Indonesia first, cultural fit
- **Transparent:** Semua on-chain, semua public
- **Playful:** Narrative "sampah jadi duit" ringan tapi serius
- **Trustworthy:** Security first, audit first

### Brand Voice
- Casual but professional
- Direct and honest
- Bilingual natural (bukan translate kaku)
- Teknis tapi accessible
- Humor sesekali (tidak over-forced)

## Community Growth Tactics

### Referral Program

**Mechanics:**
- Unique link per user
- Referrer gets 10% of referred user's rewards (first 30 days)
- Cap: 10k GOTT per referrer per referred
- Anti-sybil: both must be active cleaners

**Promotion:**
- Featured in dashboard
- Leaderboard of top referrers
- Monthly bonus untuk top 10

### Ambassador Program

**Benefits:**
- Monthly GOTT stipend
- Exclusive ambassador role + perks
- Early access to features
- Direct line to team

**Responsibilities:**
- Moderate community channels
- Create educational content

## Brand Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- Primary: Full color
- Secondary: Monochrome
- Favicon: Simplified icon
- Minimum size: 32x32 pixel

### Colors
- Primary: Green (cleanup, nature, value)
- Secondary: Orange (Indonesia, warmth, action)
- Accent: White/Gray (clean, neutral)
- Warning: Red (scam alert)
- Success: Teal (cleanup complete)

### Typography
- Headers: Modern sans-serif (Inter, Space Grotesk)
- Body: Readable sans-serif (Inter, Roboto)
- Code: Monospace (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code)

### Do's
✅ Use bahasa Indonesia naturally
✅ Show real cleanup results (before/after)
✅ Emphasize transparency (on-chain data)
✅ Be honest about limitations
✅ Credit community contributions

### Don'ts
❌ No financial advice
❌ No price predictions
❌ No "moonshot" language
❌ No bashing competitors directly
❌ No fake testimonials

## Conclusion

Marketing GOTT = **local-first, utility-driven, community-owned.**

Jangan meniru cara marketing meme coin. Jangan ride pure hype. Bangun credibility via real product, real community, real transparency.

**"Marketing terbaik untuk GOTT adalah user yang puas cleanup wallet-nya sendiri."**
